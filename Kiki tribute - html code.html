A Kiki Tribute

A life well lived

Kiki was the bestest of cats and we will miss her all dearly.

Some facts about Kiki:

  • She was the speakiest of cats (gaining the name speaky Kiki)
  • She had little tiny white boots (Kiki boots)
  • She had a beautiful sister named Hiro
  • She didn't always see eye to eye with Loki, but they still liked each other.
  • She once wore glasses
  • She would cuddle you when you were sad
  • If you entered a room you had to stroke her or she wouldn't stop meowing
